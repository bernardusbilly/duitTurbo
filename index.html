How It Works

Application Process

  • 1. Download App from Google Play
  • 2. Register a new account using your email
  • 3. Fill application form and upload required document
  • 4. If your application is approved, your fund will be disbursed within 15 minutes.

Repayment Process

  • 1. Make payment through your nearest ATM with the amount as stated on your account at the day of payment
  • 2. Choose “Transaksi Lainnya” - “Transfer” - Transfer ke Bank lain
  • 3. Fill bank code and your virtual account number
  • 4. Enter payment amount and finish transaction
