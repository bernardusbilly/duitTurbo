Loan Structure

  • Initial loan size is 350.000 or 500.000 Rupiah
  • Length of loan varies from 5-30 days
  • There is an initial fee of 5.000 Rupiah regardless of your loan size
  • The loan fee is 3.500 Rupiah per day for loan size of 350.000 Rupiah and 5.000 Rupiah per day for loan size of 500.000 Rupiah
  • Repayment can be done all at once or installed up to three times with a 5000 rupiah fee per payment transaction.
  • Late fee is 10.000 Rupiah per day for loan size of 350.000 Rupiah and 20.000 Rupiah per day for loan size of 500.000 Rupiah
  • If you have an exceptional credit performance, you may qualify for a larger loan size in the future.

Reward Structure

  • Every time you invite a friend using your unique code, you obtain 1 point.
  • If your friend apply for a loan, you obtain an additional 2 points.
  • If your friend repays on time, you obtain 10 points
  • If your friend is late on payment, you will be penalized 1 point/late day
  • If your friend fails to pay or is proven to act in a fraudulent way, you will be penalized 30 points.
  • Every 100 points, you will be rewarded 20.000 rupiah.
